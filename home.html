Welcome to Mercy Thai

Here at Mercy Thai we have proudly been serving authentic Thai cuisine to the Greater Omaha Area since ____. Our restaurant is family-owned and operated and we welcome you to enjoy your dining experience with us.

Along with some Thai classics, our menu boasts a handful of original and unique dishes created and perfected over the years by our amazing chefs.

Feast your eyes



  • Check out our Shrimp Pad Thai! Pad Thai is an obvious customer favorite. Pick from your choice of meet or vegetables, then a spice level from 1-10.



  • Did someone say Drunken Noodles? Easily one of our most recommended dishes, people keep coming back for this one!


  • Don't forget to order some appetizers to start yourself off! These Golden Triangles are very popular, but try our Crab Rangoon and Fried Dumplings!
